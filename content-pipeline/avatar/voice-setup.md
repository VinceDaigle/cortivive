# Voice / TTS Setup Guide

## Recommended Free Tools

### Option 1: TTS Vibes (Primary)
- Free, high-quality voices
- Multiple natural-sounding female voices
- Adjustable speed and pitch
- No watermark on audio

### Option 2: Countik
- Free TikTok-optimized TTS
- Familiar TikTok voice styles
- Quick generation
- Good for short-form content

### Option 3: Record Your Own Voice
- Most authentic option
- Use a phone with a quiet room
- Record in Voice Memos or Audacity (free)
- Consistent tone matters more than perfect audio

## Voice Selection Criteria

Pick ONE voice and use it for every video:
- Female, natural tone
- Age range: 30-45 sounding
- Conversational speed (not robotic)
- Warm, empathetic tone
- American English (or your target market accent)

## Script Preparation for TTS

1. Write in short sentences — TTS handles them better
2. Add commas for natural pauses
3. Use "..." for dramatic pauses
4. Spell out numbers: "three" not "3"
5. Avoid abbreviations: "doctor" not "Dr."
6. Read the script aloud first to check flow
7. Test one paragraph before generating the full script
