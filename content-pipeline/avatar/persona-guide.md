# AI Persona Guide

## Character Profile

- **Age**: ~40 years old
- **Look**: Relatable, natural, not "influencer perfect"
- **Style**: Casual — t-shirts, hoodies, leggings, minimal makeup
- **Setting**: Home environment — kitchen, living room, bedroom
- **Progression**: Early videos show visible stress (puffy face, tired eyes, bloating). Over weeks, gradual transformation — less bloated, calmer, more energized.

## Consistency Rules

1. **Same face in every video** — use the exact same base image/seed for the AI character
2. **Same voice** — pick one TTS voice and never change it
3. **Same color palette** — warm, muted tones (not bright/flashy)
4. **Same text style** — use the same font and caption style in CapCut
5. **Same intro energy** — conversational, like talking to a friend

## Character Arc (30-Day Story)

**Week 1 (Days 1-7)**: Discovery phase
- Character looks tired, stressed
- Expressing frustration with past failures
- "I just learned about cortisol belly and everything clicked"

**Week 2 (Days 8-14)**: Early wins
- Slightly less puffy face
- More energy in voice/delivery
- "The bloating is already going down"

**Week 3 (Days 15-21)**: Building confidence
- Visibly calmer demeanor
- Sharing specific measurements/changes
- "I can't believe this is working"

**Week 4 (Days 22-30)**: Transformation
- Clear visual difference from Day 1
- Energetic, hopeful
- "If I can do this, you can too"

## Voice & Tone

- **Speak like a friend**, not a doctor or influencer
- **Be vulnerable**: "I was embarrassed to wear a swimsuit"
- **Be specific**: "I lost 2 inches" not "I lost weight"
- **Be honest about struggles**: "Day 3 was really hard"
- **Never be preachy**: Share your experience, don't lecture
