## Best Practices

1. **Caption length**: 2-4 lines max. TikTok cuts off long captions.
2. **Hashtags**: 5-8 per post. Mix broad (#wellness) with niche (#cortisolbelly).
3. **CTA placement**: Always mention "bio" or "link" — keep it natural.
4. **Emojis**: 1-2 max. Don't overdo it.
5. **First line matters**: TikTok shows only the first line before "...more"
6. **Engagement hooks**: Ask questions to boost comments, which boost reach.
